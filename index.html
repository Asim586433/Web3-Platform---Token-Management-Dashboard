About Web3 Platform

Learn more about our platform and how to use it

What is Web3 Platform?

Web3 Platform is a user-friendly interface for interacting with the Ethereum blockchain. Our platform allows you to manage your digital assets, send and receive tokens, and view your transaction history—all in one secure location.

Key Features

Secure Wallet Connection

Connect your wallet securely using MetaMask or WalletConnect

Token Transactions

Send and receive ERC-20 and ERC-721 tokens with ease

Portfolio Overview

Track your assets and transaction history in one dashboard

Getting Started

To begin using Web3 Platform:

  1. Connect your wallet using the button in the top right corner
  2. View your dashboard to see your balance and recent transactions
  3. Use the "Send Tokens" page to transfer assets to other wallets
  4. Explore your NFT collection in the assets section

Security Notice

Your security is our top priority. We never store your private keys or seed phrases. All transactions are signed directly through your connected wallet. Always verify transaction details before confirming.

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues or have questions about using the platform, please check our documentation or contact our support team at support@web3platform.example.

Documentation

Welcome to the Web3 Platform documentation. Here you'll find everything you need to get started with our platform.

Getting Started

To begin using Web3 Platform, you'll need to connect a wallet. We support MetaMask and WalletConnect.

Wallet Connection

Follow these steps to connect your wallet:

  1. Click the "Connect Wallet" button in the top right corner
  2. Select your preferred wallet provider
  3. Approve the connection request in your wallet
  4. You're now connected and can access all platform features

Sending Tokens

To send tokens to another address:

  1. Navigate to the "Send Tokens" page
  2. Enter the recipient's wallet address
  3. Select the token type (ERC-20 or ERC-721)
  4. Choose the specific token and amount/token ID
  5. Review the transaction details and confirm

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my wallet?

Click the "Connect Wallet" button in the top right corner and select your preferred wallet provider. Follow the prompts to complete the connection.

Which wallets are supported?

We currently support MetaMask and WalletConnect. WalletConnect allows you to connect with many popular mobile wallets.

What tokens can I send?

You can send any ERC-20 tokens (like ETH, DAI, USDC) or ERC-721 tokens (NFTs) that are in your connected wallet.

Are there any fees?

We don't charge any platform fees. You'll only pay the standard Ethereum network gas fees for transactions.

Is my wallet secure?

Yes. We never have access to your private keys or seed phrases. All transactions are signed directly in your wallet.
